My First Layout

Task description: Choose a design-related subject. It can be pretty much anything. A specific designer, a specific field of design or just a piece of art you enjoy. Historical or contemporary. Write a text of your own about your selected topic. The text should be written as if you were an author writing for a coffee table book on art/design. This is not an academic paper. Design a minimum of two spreads, maximum of four (4-8 pages) about your topic intended for a coffee table book about design. For this assignment it is permitted to borrow pictures from the internet.
