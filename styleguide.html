Branding

Task description: Create a visual identity/style guide for your selected company. The style guide (handed in as a PDF) should be done as 12 pages with the following content:

  • Cover page
  • Spread 1 (page 2-3): Table of Contents
  • Spread 2 (page 4-5): Presentation of the company
  • Spread 3 (page 6-7): Presentation of the company logo + logo usage guidelines.
  • Spread 4 (page 8-9): Presentation of the company typography, brand colours and other graphic elements (such as patterns and textures).
  • Spread 5 (page 10-11): A mood board displaying the intended style of photos and other visuals for the brand.
  • Back cover: use for whatever you find most suitable
